Dragon Ball Super Storyline

  • Dragon Ball Super's storyline takes place after the defeat of Majin Buu in Dragon Ball Z, during the ten-year time skip to the 28th World Martial Arts Tournament. Here's a breakdown of the major arcs:

  • Battle of Gods Arc
    Beerus, the God of Destruction from Universe 7, awakens from a long slumber and seeks out the "Super Saiyan God" mentioned in a prophecy.Goku and his friends try to appease Beerus, but eventually, Goku transforms into a Super Saiyan God to fight him.Despite Goku's power-up, he is still defeated by Beerus, who decides to spare Earth for the time being, intrigued by the Saiyans.

  • Resurrection 'F' Arc
    Sorbet and Tagoma, remnants of Frieza's army, use the Dragon Balls to revive their leader.Frieza trains intensely and returns to Earth seeking revenge against Goku.Goku and Vegeta, who have been training with Whis (Beerus's attendant), confront Frieza, who achieves a new Golden form.Goku attains the Super Saiyan Blue transformation, but Frieza manages to destroy the Earth (though Whis reverses time, allowing Goku to defeat him).

    • Universe 6 Arc:
    Champa, Beerus's twin brother and the God of Destruction of Universe 6, proposes a tournament between the strongest fighters of their respective universes.The winning universe will exchange planets with the loser. Goku and Vegeta join Team Universe 7, along with Piccolo, Gohan, and Monaka (who is secretly weak). They face strong opponents from Universe 6, including Hit, a powerful assassin with the ability to manipulate time. Univere 7 wins the tournament, but the exchange of planets is ultimately negated by the Omni-King Zeno.
  • Future Trunks Arc:
    Future Trunks returns to the present timeline with news of a powerful new enemy called Goku Black, who looks exactly like Goku and is causing devastation in his timeline.Goku, Vegeta, and Trunks travel to the future to confront him. They discover that Goku Black is actually Zamasu, a Kaioshin from Universe 10, who has stolen Goku's body and desires to eradicate all mortals. Zamasu merges with his future self, becoming an immortal and incredibly powerful being. Despite their combined efforts, Goku and Vegeta are unable to defeat Merged Zamasu. Future Trunks, with the energy of all remaining life on Earth, manages to slice Zamasu in half, but his influence persists. Ultimately, Goku summons the Omni-King Zeno from the present, who erases the entire future timeline, including Zamasu. Future Trunks and Mai then depart to a new, unaltered timeline.

  • Universe Survival Arc:
    The Omni-Kings Zeno and Future Zeno decide to hold a tournament of power between eight universes. The losing universes will be erased from existence. Goku gathers a team of ten fighters from Universe 7, including Gohan, Piccolo, Vegeta, Frieza, Android 17 and 18, Krillin, Master Roshi, and Tien Shinhan. They face formidable opponents from other universes in a massive battle royale. The arc focuses on themes of survival, teamwork, and pushing one's limits. Goku achieves the Ultra Instinct form during the tournament.In the end, Universe 7 emerges victorious thanks to Android 17's selfless wish on the Super Dragon Balls, restoring all the erased universes.
